Wizard

The Wizard, also known as M. Rasmodius, is a villager who lives in the Wizard's Tower to the far west of Cindersap Forest. Wizard studies the spirit world from his tower in cindersap forest. He is fluent in many elemental languages. At any friendship level greater than zero friendship points, you may receive a gift in the mail from the Wizard. The chance of receiving a gift in the mail increases as your friendship with the Wizard increases.

Schedule

The Wizard does not leave the Wizard's Tower (except for festivals and a cutscene), and can be found there when the tower is accessible (6am to 11pm). After the player has unlocked the Witch's Hut, he can be found in his tower after 11pm by using the warp spot in the Witch's Hut.
