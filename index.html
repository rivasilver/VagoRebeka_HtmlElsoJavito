Kik azok a Junimók?

A Junimók erdei lények, amelyek az elhanyagolt Közösségi Központban találtak ottont. The Player can complete Bundles for the Junimos by collecting and offering various items found in the Valley. When a bundle is completed, a Junimo will come and collect it, then stash it away in the hut in the main room of the Community Center. Completing a bundle will reward the player with an item. Completing all bundles in a room will reward the player with an improvement to the valley, and a star toward Community Center completion. After placing a star, a Junimo will follow the player around until the player leaves the community center. After all stars have been placed, the Community Center will be repaired, and the Junimos will return to the forest.

Hogyan lehet Junimónk?

A játékos megvásárolhat egy Junimó Kuckót a Varázslótól, miután elvégezték a Goblin Probléma küldetést. Elhelyezése után a junimók a kuckóban fognak lakni a játékos farmján. They'll automatically harvest any ripe crops within range of the hut and store them inside, where they can be gathered by the player at any time.
